About Our Volleyball Program

The WSU Volleyball Team is a competitive and energetic squad that proudly represents the university in regional and national tournaments. Our program offers:

  • Expert coaching from experienced volleyball professionals
  • Specialized training for all positions including setters, spikers, liberos, and blockers
  • Regular participation in inter-university leagues and championships
  • Access to high-quality indoor and outdoor volleyball courts
  • Strength, agility, and endurance training programs
  • Academic and personal development support for student-athletes

For more information, contact our Volleyball Coordinator at volleyball@wsu.ac.za or call +27 47 502 2846.
