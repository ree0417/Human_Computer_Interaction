About Our Athletics Program

The WSU Athletics Team competes in university leagues and tournaments across the region. Our program includes:

  • Professional coaching from experienced track and field specialists
  • Specialized training programs for sprinters, distance runners, and field athletes
  • Competitions against top university teams
  • Access to state-of-the-art athletic facilities
  • Strength and conditioning programs
  • Academic support tailored for student athletes

For more information, contact our Athletics Coordinator at athletics@wsu.ac.za or call +27 47 502 2846.
