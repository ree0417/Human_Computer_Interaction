About Our Soccer Program

The WSU Soccer Team competes in university leagues and tournaments across the region. Our program includes:

  • Professional coaching and training sessions
  • Competitive matches against other universities
  • Access to sports facilities and equipment
  • Team building and leadership opportunities
  • Academic support for student athletes

For more information, contact our Sports Department at sports@wsu.ac.za or call +27 47 502 2844.
